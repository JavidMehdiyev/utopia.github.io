Hour of Code

Hour Of Code - was a second homework , which actually was one of the most difficult tasks. Students were divided into the groups of 3-5 people. The main goal of the homework was to find the school, prepare the presentation about the basics of programming, and to explain what is programming to the pupils of the school. Additionally, we presented a presentation about ADA University, where we encouraged pupils showing the quality of education and positive feedback of students about university.
